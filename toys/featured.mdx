## Featured Toy Controls

All featured toys include carefully designed control surfaces:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Starter Presets">
    Featured toys ship with **starter presets** that bundle optimal settings for first-time users. Look for the preset button in the toy's control panel.
    
    Example presets:
    - **Aurora Painter**: "aurora starter" - Balanced glow and speed tuned for first look delight
    - **Bioluminescent Tidepools**: "guided glow starter" - Higher glow with balanced trail and current speed
    - **Neon Wave**: "soft glow starter" - Reduced bloom intensity for a gentler first look
    - **Spiral Burst**: "beat pop starter" - Higher beat sensitivity with punchy pulse bursts
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wow Controls">
    Each featured toy highlights a **"wow control"**—the single control that delivers the most immediate, delightful change.
    
    Example wow controls:
    - **Aurora Painter**: Quick preset · Starter
    - **Bioluminescent Tidepools**: Glow strength
    - **Spiral Burst**: Beat sensitivity
    - **Bubble Harmonics**: Split sensitivity
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Gestural Interactions">
    Many featured toys support **pinch/rotate gestures** and **arrow-key mood switching** for dynamic, hands-on control:
    
    - **Pinch**: Scale or zoom visual elements
    - **Rotate**: Twist or spin visual elements
    - **Arrow keys**: Cycle through preset moods or visual modes
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
